Kansas City

I am from Kansas City and I love everything this city has to offer. I have lived here ever since I was about 6 years old and I don't get sick of it. We have some of the best barbeque in the country from places such as Arthur Bryant's, Gate's, and Jack Stack BBQ. There is always something to do as well like going to try new pop up bars, attending a music festival, go to one of the sports teams games and we have a lot of lakes around the city for hanging out or just relaxing!
